ABOUT

Nicedoodle is leveraging the NFT marketplace to fund worthy causes. Using celebrity status to make a difference through the world of crypto tokens. Founded by Dimitri Rodokanakis as a project, hoping to raise significant funding for charities across there globe. Every Nicedoodle NFT will be auctioned off to the highest bidder and the proceeds will fund one of our selected approved charities.

MISSION

Challenging celebrities to use their power and following status to raise crypto currency for good. Digital collectibles are at fever pitch level and we want to harness this energy. We wish to encourage other leading charity organisations to open up to cryptocurrency donations creating an even wider net to collect funds.

PURPOSE

The purpose is simple, to help those in need. The vessel is the NFT market and the generosity comes from those we follow on social media and of course the collectors.

How it works

We request a celebrity to draw a doodle of literally anything. It could be a dot on a page or a David Choe work of art. The artwork then gets shipped to one of our secret locations, minted as a Nicedoodle and auctioned to the highest bidder. Proceeds go to one of the charities that we are working with or one that the celebrity nominates.
